Our Story

One year ago, the students at Kister Homeschool were on the outside looking in. There was no school debate program, no team, and no obvious path to competitive debate. That changed when Bubby spotted an article about Citron Online, founded by Alex Libkind on the belief that debate should be accessible to everyone.

Coach Mike at Citron was our point of contact from the start — he worked with NSDA to establish our homeschool status and onboarded the kids into the program. He taught them the fundamentals: how to flow an argument, how to find and evaluate evidence, how to cut cards, and how to organize a case. Coach Batya worked with them on Speech and Congressional debate, including the finer points of delivery and presentation. Citron set up practice rounds with other schools and guided the kids through a full season of online competition.

The qualification to NSDA Nationals in Public Forum, the kids’ first season in the format, is a direct reflection of that coaching.
