Our Prep

Our debaters are homeschoolers — there was no school program, no team, no obvious path to competitive debate. That changed when Bubbe spotted an article about Citron Online, founded by Alex Libkind on the belief that debate should be accessible to everyone. Citron didn’t just open the door — they walked us through it. They worked with NSDA to establish our homeschool status, made their full coaching staff available for online sessions — including coaches Batya, Mercedes, and Mike — set up practice rounds with other schools, and guided the kids through a full season of online competition.

Coach Mike in particular shaped not just how the kids debate, but how they think — how to build an argument, how to listen to an opponent, how to stay composed under pressure. The qualification to NSDA Nationals in Public Forum, the kids’ first season in the format, is a direct reflection of that coaching.

This page is where we organize the preparation for Richmond.
