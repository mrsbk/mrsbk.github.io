♬ The Origins of musiKLASK ▼

It started with an argument. SK, GM of the County Cougars, and YK, GM of the Garden State Guardians, were debating the age-old question: who was the greater composer — Bach or Marcello? Words weren't settling it. So SK proposed they do what any reasonable person would do: play a game of KLASK. Winner's composer wins the argument.

SK won. Bach won. And in that moment, musiKLASK was born — a league where composers don't just live on in concert halls, but compete on the KLASK board, their legacies riding on every goal scored and every magnet snapped.
